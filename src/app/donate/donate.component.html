Can I donate stock or make my donation by wire transfer?

Yes, we accept donations of stock and donations by domestic and international wire transfer. Please email donate@SkillWareacademy.org for our wire and stock transfer information.

Do you accept employer matches?

Yes, we do. You'll find more information on employer matched gifts here.

Can I donate in a currency other than US dollar?

We only accept donations in US dollars. We cannot accept foreign cash or foreign checks. Since donations made using PayPal are automatically converted to US dollars, supporters outside the United States may want to donate using this method.

What is the learners fund?

The Learners Fund is a select community of supporters who make unrestricted gifts of $1,000 to $99,999 each year. Interested in joining the Learners Fund and changing lives for millions of learners each month in more than 190 countries? Email us at donate@SkillWareacademy.org to learn more.
